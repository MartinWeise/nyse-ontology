New York Stock Exchange Ontology

Student project as part of the course 188.387 Semi-Automatic Information and Knowledge Systems at Technical University of Vienna.

Purpose

All of the contributors have strong computer science background. In order to learn more about other domains, we model the stock exchange ontology from the economical domain. The purpose is therefore defined as allowing amateurs to quickly learn more about the interdependencies of the stock market and allow them to draw (rudimental) conclusions based on publicly available datasets.

Alignment

We aligned our ontolgy with others:

  • stock_data.owl (Github)
  • VirtualMarketPlaceV4.owl (Github)
  • finance_th_web.owl (Github)
  • stockexchange.owl (Github)
  • nasdaq-ont.owl (Github)
  • protonu.owl (GitLab)
